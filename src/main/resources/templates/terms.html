HIPAA Compliance

Protecting your health information rights

1. HIPAA Overview

AXES Hospital is fully committed to HIPAA compliance and protecting your Protected Health Information (PHI). We adhere to strict federal guidelines regarding data privacy.

2. Your HIPAA Rights

Under HIPAA, you have the right to:

  • Access and obtain copies of your medical records
  • Request amendments to your health information
  • Request restrictions on use and disclosure of your PHI
  • Request confidential communications

Our Commitment

We maintain comprehensive technical, physical, and administrative safeguards to ensure the confidentiality of your health data.
